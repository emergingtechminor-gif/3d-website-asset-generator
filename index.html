About the Minor

Embrace
the Future

The Emerging Tech Minor + Lab at Inholland University of Applied Sciences is designed specifically for creative and business-minded students who want to understand and apply the technologies shaping our world.

No technical experience required. This minor is for curious minds from all study backgrounds — together we explore how AI, XR, Robotics, and IoT are reshaping creative and business contexts.

Alongside the minor, the Emerging Tech Lab runs applied research for the creative industries, building knowledge that feeds back into both education and practice.

Research & Education Hub

Emerging Tech
Lab

The Emerging Tech Lab is a strategic research and innovation hub at Inholland University of Applied Sciences. Our mission is to inform and inspire educational programs on the possibilities of emerging technology, identify field-specific needs, and execute impactful projects through a global network of partners.

🔬

Strategic Research

Aiding research centres in utilizing emerging tech as a research method, tool, and object of research — fostering experimentation that is informed by research and manifested through practice.

🎓

Educational Innovation

Educating staff on integrating emerging tech as pedagogical tools to enhance student learning trajectories, and supporting the creation of content for creative and business-related ends.

🤝

Collaborative Execution

Conceptualizing and executing long-term projects, aiding in grant writing, and acting as a linking pin between international educational institutions and industry partners.

Projects &

Research · Immersive Heritage

Dutch Graffiti Library On Tour

A research project digitally preserving Dutch graffiti culture using Gaussian splatting (3D imaging), VR, and AR. Co-creative design sessions with young people build interactive experiences, with a replicable toolkit for cultural institutions across Europe.

Sept 2025 – Sept 2026 · Partners: Dutch Graffiti Library, OBA Next Lab, European Cultural Foundation, Amsterdam Immersive Alliance · Funded by The Europe Challenge

Read more ↗
Research · CoE CI

Designing Immersive Experiences for Connectedness & Belonging

Research exploring how VR and AR can strengthen social connection in an increasingly individualized world. Collaborates with dance company Another Kind of Blue to prototype XR experiences centred on empathy, inclusivity, and collective engagement.

Led by Dr. Pawan Bhansing · Team: Shant Bayramian, Mark Jacobs · Partner: Another Kind of Blue

Read more ↗
Research · Grant Pending

NWO Start — VR Storytelling for Postcolonial Narratives

A proposed research project investigating how VR can be used as a medium for postcolonial storytelling — exploring immersive narrative formats that center underrepresented historical perspectives.

Grant application pending
Research · Network · IX Labs

Associated Partner — IX Labs (CIIIC)

The Emerging Tech Lab is an associated partner of IX Labs — a nationwide Dutch network of facilities for research and innovation in immersive experiences. The connected hubs provide access to VR headsets, AR devices, motion capture, and 360° cameras, serving creators, businesses, researchers, and educational institutions.

About IX Labs ↗
Education · Accreditation

Accreditation — TM & CB

The Lab supported accreditation for the Tourism Management and Creative Business programmes by developing immersive experiences — including Gaussian splatting environments and 360° content — to present the programmes and their physical locations to external accreditation committees.

Education · Programme

XR Production Lab Week — Creative Business

The Lab hosts an intensive one-week pressure cooker in XR for 2nd year Creative Business students. Students go from concept to prototype in immersive media — building hands-on experience in VR/AR production under sprint conditions.

Education · International

Hackathon with Kolding Design School

The Lab collaborates with Kolding Design School (Denmark) to organise a cross-border hackathon between Emerging Tech Minor students and Kolding students — bringing together creative and tech perspectives for rapid innovation challenges.

Education · Competition · 🥇 Gold

Emerging Technologies Olympiad — Hong Kong 2025

Inholland students won first place at the Emerging Technologies Olympiad 2025 in Hong Kong, competing against 40+ international teams with AI, XR, and machine learning solutions. The Emerging Tech Lab facilitated and coached the winning teams: Team Betternative (Gold — machine vision for sustainable fashion), Team Aithos (Bronze), and Team HoloMed (Award of Merit).

Read more ↗
Education · Industry Event

Immersive Tech Week Rotterdam

The Lab and XR Production Lab partnered with Immersive Tech Week Rotterdam to advance XR education. 100 students and 10 staff participated, with students placing second in a national hackathon and delivering six presentations from the XR Production Lab stage.

Read more ↗
Education · Event

DigiQuest — The Future of Work

A two-day Inholland event exploring how AI, AR, and blockchain will shape the future workplace. The Lab and Minor helped with organisation and event development, bringing together industry partners including KLM, ABN AMRO, BTG, and OpenAI Talks for students and educators.

Read more ↗
Education · Institutional

AI-Ready Faculty Support

The Lab provides strategic and educational support to the faculty-wide AI network at Inholland's Faculty of Creative Business — helping the institution develop coherent AI literacy frameworks, tools, and teaching strategies across all programmes.

Education · Open Access

Open Learning Materials in XR & AI

The Lab has developed open learning materials in XR and AI — available for universities around the world to use freely.

Education · Industry

Digital Humans for Education

The Lab developed a range of digital human formats for educational ends: 2D AI presenter videos using Synthesia, fully interactive 3D avatars, and 360° VR quiz experiences — enabling scalable, immersive learning content for students and educators alike.

The Emerging Tech Minor + Lab is open to students from all study backgrounds — no technical experience required. We welcome curious, motivated students who want to shape the future of creative industries.

Eligibility — you can apply if you are:

  • An Inholland student from any faculty or programme
  • A Kies op Maat student from another Dutch university
  • An international student from a partner university
  • In your 3rd year or beyond
  • Able to commit to full-time study (30 EC)
  • Comfortable studying in English
