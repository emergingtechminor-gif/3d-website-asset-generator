What We Do

A Lab
for the Future

The Emerging Technologies Minor at Inholland is an international programme for curious minds from all study backgrounds — no technical experience required.

We explore how technologies like AI, XR, Blockchain, Robotics and IoT are reshaping the world — and how to apply them in creative, social and business contexts.

The Lab drives applied research alongside education, producing knowledge that feeds directly back into industry and teaching practice.

"We want students to become resilient for the future. The minor aligns with digital literacy and lifelong learning principles."

— Shant Bayramian, Co-founder

Infrastructure

The Lab

The Emerging Tech Lab is a research and education hub embedded in the Inholland Sluislab ecosystem in Amsterdam's Sluisbuurt neighbourhood — connecting students directly with community and industry.

Beyond education, the Lab runs applied research in creative industries, contributing to the national knowledge infrastructure and publishing actionable insights for practitioners.

  • High-end workstations with GPU compute for AI & generative models
  • VR headsets and full motion capture suite
  • Robotics kits, robotic arms, and Raspberry Pi systems
  • 3D printers and rapid prototyping hardware
  • Unreal Engine & real-time rendering pipelines
  • Industry-licensed software ecosystem
Amsterdam Immersive Alliance CoE CI Network IX Labs ARC Research Center
